OUTREACH

In keeping with the SEDS' evergreen attempt to spread awareness, our members were also involved in the inaugration of a SEDS chapter at Karpagam university, marking a very proud day in SEDS India's calendar in furthering the SEDS umbrella in South India. The Outreach is responsible for organizing sessions in various schools forming space clubs as part of the "OneSpace" initiative where the kids are taught about basics regarding space starting with why astronomy is important to us till the big bang theory. It covers all the myriad topics which are taught keeping the age and prior knowledge of children in mind. The presenters are also a group of SEDS members who are also space enthusiasts.
